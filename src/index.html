Welcome to the Breaking Bad Universe!

Breaking Bad is an American television series created and produced by Vince Giligan. Set and produced in Albuquerque, New Mexico, follows the story of Walter White, a chemistry professor who is struggling with a recent diagnosis of lung cancer. Walter turns to a life of crime producing and distributing methamphetamine alongside a former student, Jesse Pinkman, in order to raise enough money for his family's future after his inevitable death. Better call Saul coexists with the Breaking Bad universe. This series tell the story of the lawyer from the Breaking Bad series, Saul Goodman.
